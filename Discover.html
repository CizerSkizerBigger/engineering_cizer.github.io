Discover Your Potential

Are you wondering which engineering major is the perfect fit for you? We’ve created a quiz to help you figure it out.

There are two versions: a short one with 50 questions and a longer one with 100 questions. Both are completely free to take and you can retake them as many times as you’d like. You will be given a topic and asked to rate your interest in it on a scale of 1 to 5, with 1 being the least interesting and 5 being the most interesting.

It is important to note that. this test measures interest in a particular field and may change as you develop into a real engineer. Regardless, this test serves as an initial compatibility testing.
